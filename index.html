Grilled Cheese Sandwich with Ham and Tomato

change content for web dev advanced

Ingredients:

  • Ham
  • Cheddar cheese slices
  • Butter
  • Tomato slices
  • 2 slices of bread

Instructions:

1. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.

2. Place a slice of cheddar cheese on the unbuttered side of one slice of bread.

3. Layer the ham and tomato slices on top of the cheese.

4. Top with the second slice of bread, buttered side up.

5. Heat a skillet over medium heat.

6. Place the sandwich in the skillet and cook until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 3-4 minutes per side.

7. Slice and serve hot.
